简历 Resume

1996年7月毕业于徐州医学院临床医学系，毕业后从事神经内科临床、教学和科研工作。2003年5月赴美留学，并于2007年12月获得东田纳西州大学医学院生物医学博士学位，同年9月开始在Emory大学医学院接受博士后训练。2010年3月加入苏州大学神经科学研究所。多年来，致力于神经科疾病特别是脑血管病的研究，并取得了很多的科研成果，研究了神经肽对小鼠脑缺血损伤的保护作用，并探讨了神经细胞的死亡机制，包括necroptosis的发生机制。 近年来研究了与神经发育疾病Joubert综合症发生密切相关的蛋白Ahi1的功能，成功培育出条件性Ahi1基因敲除小鼠，发现Ahi1蛋白功能异常与抑郁样症状的发生关系密切。相关成果发表在PNAs，Stroke，Journal of Neurochemistry, Glia, Human Molecular Genetics等著名杂志，并被世界一流杂志如Cell, Nature Chemical Biology等多次引用。徐兴顺教授是美国神经科学学会，神经毒理学会的会员，为Neurotoxicity Research杂志编委，为10余个国际杂志的审稿人。由于在神经病学领域的长期工作受到相关同行的认可，成为江苏省神经病学神经遗传组副组长和脑血管组委员。2013年被江苏省卫生厅聘为江苏省特聘医学专家，同时入选江苏省创新创业人才计划，2015年入选苏州市姑苏卫生人才计划并获得江苏省科技进步奖三等奖。

Dr. Xingshun Xu graduated from Xuzhou Medical College in July 1996. After graduation, he worked as a neurologist in the Affiliated Hospital of Xuzhou Medical College. In May 2003, he went to the USA and received his PhD from East Tennessee State University in December 2007. He got his postdoctoral training at Emory University from September 2007. In March 2010, he joined the Institute of Neuroscience at Soochow University.

Dr. Xu’s research interests focused on neurological diseases, especially stroke. During his PhD studies, he examined the neuroprotection of different agents on cerebral ischemia injury and explored the mechanisms of cell death including necroptosis, a newly identified programmed cell death. In his postdoctoral training, he explored the function of Ahi1, a Joubert syndrome-related protein. He found that Ahi1 is involved in the regulation of recycling and sorting of TrkB receptor and Ahi1 deficiency causes depression-like phenotype in mice. His research findings were published in several journals such as PNAs，Stroke，Journal of Neurochemistry, Glia, Human Molecular Genetics and so on. Dr. Xu was appointed Distinguished medical experts, also selected innovation and entrepreneurship program in Jiangsu Province at 2013. In 2015, he got the Science and Technology Progress Award of Jiangsu Province.
